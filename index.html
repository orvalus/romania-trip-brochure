Why Romania

Europe before the crowds

Castles set in forested mountains. Roads that cross the Carpathians. Medieval towns that remain lived-in, not staged.

Romania offers a version of Europe that feels both authentic and undiscovered.

Signature Experiences

Journey highlights

Royal grandeur

Peleș Castle

Imperial architecture in the heart of the Carpathians. Opulent interiors and a mountain view that captures your soul.

Legend & myth

Bran Castle

Dramatic, gothic, and full of stories. A fortress best understood over wine than any guidebook.

Medieval rhythm

Brașov

Elegant squares, cobblestone streets, and an urban pace that never rushes.

Village authenticity

Viscri

A UNESCO World Heritage site where traditions feel natural, not performed. Century-old houses and Saxon heritage.

Citadel color

Sighișoara

Vibrant, living, and intensely atmospheric. A medieval citadel that pulses with energy. (UNESCO World Heritage)

Saxon elegance

Sibiu

Refined Transylvanian architecture, spacious squares, and cultural sophistication that captivates.

02
Day 1 · Thursday June 25

Bucharest to Sinaia to Brașov

Early morning departure from Bucharest. Arrival in Sinaia for a visit to Peleș Castle — refined, intimate, and set in the forested foothills of the Carpathians.

Continue through the Prahova Valley to Brașov. Arrival in the Old Town and time to settle in before dinner.

Lunch
Kuib (Sinaia) — Light seasonal lunch, perfect reset after arrival
Dinner
Dei Frati (Brașov) — Elegant first dinner, one of Brașov's best tables
Stay
Radisson Blu Aurum (Brașov)
Peleș Castle · scenic drive · Brașov arrival
03
Day 2 · Friday June 26

Brașov & Bran

Morning exploring Brașov: medieval squares, hidden cafés, and pedestrian streets at a relaxed pace. Optional cable car to Tâmpa for panoramic views over the city and surrounding mountains.

Afternoon: Bran Castle visit — atmospheric and best experienced without rushing. Return to Brașov for a relaxed evening.

Lunch
Sub Tâmpa (Brașov) — Relaxed lunch with panoramic views
Dinner
Bistro de l'Arte (Brașov) — Intimate evening with artistic local feel
Stay
Radisson Blu Aurum (Brașov)
Brașov exploration · Bran Castle · optional nature
04
Day 3 · Saturday June 27

Viscri to Sighișoara

A transition from rural Transylvania to one of its most atmospheric towns. Morning in Viscri — a quiet Saxon village where traditions remain part of everyday life.

Continue toward Sighișoara, arriving in the medieval citadel in the afternoon. Evening within the walls, with dinner at Joseph Haydn House.

Lunch
Rural guesthouse (Viscri) — Traditional village lunch, authentic and local
Dinner
Joseph Haydn House (Sighișoara) — Intimate dinner within the medieval citadel
Stay
Fronius Residence (Sighișoara)
Viscri · citadel arrival · evening dining
05
Day 4 · Sunday June 28

Sighișoara to Biertan to Sibiu

Departure from Sighișoara after breakfast. Stop in Biertan, a fortified church set in open countryside (UNESCO World Heritage).

Continue to Sibiu — a shift toward a more refined, urban atmosphere. Arrival in the afternoon, with time to explore the Old Town.

Lunch
Casa Terra (Biertan area) — Countryside lunch, simple and local
Dinner
Kombinat (Sibiu) — Contemporary, lively modern contrast
Stay
Noblesse Boutique (Sibiu)
Biertan stop · scenic transfer · refined arrival
06
Day 5 · Monday June 29

Sibiu & Corvin Castle

A flexible day based on pace and interest. Enjoy Sibiu at leisure — cafés, galleries, quiet streets, and time to slow down.

Or take a longer excursion to Corvin Castle, one of the most dramatic Gothic sites in Eastern Europe. Evening back in Sibiu.

Lunch
Lumina (Sibiu) — Polished, central, relaxed lunch
Dinner
Kulinarium (Sibiu) — Refined final dinner, quiet closing moment
Stay
Noblesse Boutique (Sibiu)
Sibiu morning & Corvin Castle · best of both
07
Day 6 · Tuesday June 30

Transfăgărășan to Bucharest

The final day follows the Transfăgărășan — one of Europe's most scenic mountain roads. A slow ascent into the Carpathians, with time for viewpoints and a light stop at Bâlea Lac.

Descent toward Wallachia, with optional stops such as Curtea de Argeș Monastery, before continuing to Bucharest. Final evening kept relaxed near the airport.

Lunch
Bâlea Lac — Scenic mountain lunch with alpine views
Dinner
Posada Vidraru (near Vidraru Dam) — Scenic lakeside dinner, quiet and atmospheric
Stay
Hilton Garden Inn (Otopeni)
Scenic finale · mountain pass · flexible departure
